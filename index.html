Tracking COVID-19 in Pennsylvania

Created by David Miron

Intro

Last updated on, March 24, 2020

As I am quarantined in PA, I have been tracking the development of COVID-19 in the state. All data is pulled from health.pa.gov on a daily basis. All opinions are my own. This website is mainly to serve as a place for me to organize my findings, but it can also provide a source of truth for others in the state. Stay safe and healthy!

Day 3 of Tracking

Daily Update, March 24, 2020

2255 tests today. 207 positive. Tests reported per day is increasing at an average of 40% over the last 5 days. 1 more confirmed death today. Philadelphia has the most positive cases with 177. Montgomery County is a close second at 144 cases confirmed. 40 of 68 counties are now reporting.

Day 2 of Tracking

Daily Update, March 23, 2020

1796 tests today. 165 positive. 9% of all tests positive up from 8% yesterday. Monroe and Montgomery counties fasting growing positive cases with 42 positive tests today in both. 6 total deaths in PA now - 2 in Northampton county.

Day 1 of Tracking

Daily Update, March 22, 2020

1306 tests in PA today. 108 positive. 33 of 68 counties are now testing in PA. Those counties account for 86% of the population. Montgomery and Philadelphia with 2 times the positive cases than the next closest county down with 87 and 91, respectively.
